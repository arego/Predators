The Viverridae comprise

  1. Hemigalinae
    1. Genus Chrotogale
      1. Owston's palm civet(Chrotogale owstoni)
    2. GenusCynogale
      1. Otter civet (Cynogale bennettii)
    3. Genus Diplogale
      1. Hose's palm civet(Diplogale hosei)
    4. Genus Hemigalus
      1. Banded palm civet(Hemigalus derbyanus)
  2. Paradoxurinae
    1. Genus Arctictis
      1. Binturong (Arctictis binturong)
    2. GenusArctogalidia
      1. Small-toothed palm civet (Arctogalidia trivirgata)
    3. Genus Macrogalidia
      1. Sulawesi palm civet (Macrogalidia musschenbroekii)
    4. Genus Paguma
      1. Masked palm civet (Paguma larvata)
    5. Genus Paradoxurus
      1. Asian palm civet (Paradoxurus hermaphroditus) (Pallas, 1778)
      2. Golden palm civet(Paradoxurus zeylonensis) (Pallas, 1778)
      3. Golden wet-zone palm civet (Paradoxurus aureus) (Cuvier, 1822)
      4. Sri Lankan brown palm civet (Paradoxurus montanus) (Kelaart, 1852)
      5. Jerdon's palm civet (Paradoxurus jerdoni) (Blanford, 1885)
      6. Golden dry-zone palm civet (Paradoxurus stenocephalus) (Groves, 2009)
  3. Prionodontinae (see below)
    1. GenusPrionodon
      1. Banded linsang (Prionodon linsang)
      2. Spotted linsang (Prionodon pardicolor)
  4. Viverrinae
    1. Genus Civettictis
      1. African civet (Civettictis civetta)
    2. Genus Genetta
      1. Abyssinian genet (Genetta abyssinica)
      2. Angolan genet (Genetta angolensis)
      3. Bourlon's genet (Genetta bourloni)
      4. Crested servaline genet (Genetta cristata)
      5. Common genet (Genetta genetta)
      6. Johnston's genet (Genetta johnstoni)
      7. Rusty-spotted genet (Genetta maculata)
      8. Pardine genet (Genetta pardina)
      9. Aquatic genet (Genetta piscivora)
      10. King genet (Genetta poensis)
      11. Servaline genet (Genetta servalina)
      12. Haussa genet (Genetta thierryi)
      13. Cape genet (Genetta tigrina)
      14. Giant forest genet (Genetta victoriae)
    3. Genus Poiana
      1. Leighton's linsang (Poiana leightoni)
      2. LAfrican linsang (Poiana richardsonii)
    4. Genus Viverra
      1. Malabar large-spotted civet (Viverra civettina)
      2. Large-spotted civet (Viverra megaspila)
      3. Malayan civet (Viverra tangalunga)
      4. Large Indian civet (Viverra zibetha)
    5. Genus Viverricula
      1. Small Indian civet (Viverricula indica)
